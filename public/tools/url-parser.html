Frequently Asked Questions
Are percent-encoded characters decoded?

Yes — %20 → space and all other percent-encoded sequences are decoded in the display.

Does it support data: URIs?

data: URIs are parsed but the data payload is not decoded.

What does URL Parser &amp; Builder Online – Parse &amp; Construct URLs Free do?

URL Parser &amp; Builder Online – Parse &amp; Construct URLs Free runs entirely in your browser and helps you complete this task without uploading data to a server.
