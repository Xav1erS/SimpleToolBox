Frequently Asked Questions
How accurate is the income tax estimate?

Uses 2024 US federal brackets for illustration. Does not include deductions, credits, state tax, or FICA. Use as a rough estimate only.

Is my data sent anywhere?

No. All calculations run in your browser. Nothing is sent to any server.
