What is Lorem Ipsum?

Lorem ipsum is placeholder text used in design and typesetting when real content is not yet available. Its roots trace to a scrambled passage of Cicero's de Finibus Bonorum et Malorum (45 BC). Because it resembles natural Latin word distribution, it avoids reader distraction — allowing designers to focus on layout, spacing, and typography rather than content.

Today lorem ipsum is the standard filler text across web design, print mockups, UI wireframes, and software demos worldwide.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is lorem ipsum real Latin?

Mostly no. It is based on Cicero's de Finibus but scrambled and modified to make it meaningless. This is intentional — designers don't want readers parsing the content instead of evaluating the layout.

Is it safe to ship lorem ipsum to production?

No. Lorem ipsum is for development and design review only. Always replace it with real copy before a public launch — some users and search engines may notice placeholder text.

What is "Cicero" or "de Finibus"?

de Finibus Bonorum et Malorum ("On the Ends of Good and Evil") is a philosophical work by Marcus Tullius Cicero written in 45 BC. The lorem ipsum passage derives from sections 1.10.32 and 1.10.33 of this text, though heavily altered.
