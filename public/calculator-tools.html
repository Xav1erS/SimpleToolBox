Who uses these calculators?

Home buyers use the mortgage calculator to estimate monthly payments and total interest before making an offer. Students track GPA trends and plan their remaining coursework. Diners split restaurant bills and calculate fair tip amounts in seconds.

All calculations run entirely in your browser — no numbers, names, or financial details are ever sent to a server.

Common calculator workflows

Home buying — use Mortgage Calculator to see monthly P&I, then Savings Calculator to plan how long it takes to build a down payment.

Personal finance — compare loan offers with Loan Calculator, then use Discount Calculator to evaluate sale prices on big purchases.

Health planning — calculate BMI, then use Calorie Calculator to set daily intake targets based on your activity level and goal.
