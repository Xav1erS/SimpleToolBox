What is Crop Image?

Crop Image lets you trim a picture down to the exact area you need. It is useful for social posts, screenshots, product photos, and any image that needs a cleaner frame before publishing or sharing.

How to Use Crop Image

  1. Upload an image into the crop tool.
  2. Choose a free or preset crop ratio.
  3. Adjust the crop coordinates and size until the preview looks right.
  4. Download the cropped result in your preferred format.

Frequently Asked Questions
Can I crop to a square or portrait ratio?

Yes. Use the built-in ratio presets such as 1:1, 16:9, or 4:5 to jump to common crop shapes quickly.

Do I need to drag a crop box?

No. This version focuses on precise numeric crop control so you can edit the crop area exactly and see the result immediately.

Can I download the cropped result as PNG or JPEG?

Yes. Choose the output format before downloading the cropped image.

Is the image uploaded anywhere?

No. The crop preview and export run entirely in your browser.
