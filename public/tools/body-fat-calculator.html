How to Measure for the Navy Method

  • Height — stand straight without shoes
  • Waist — for men: at the navel; for women: at the narrowest point
  • Hips (women only) — at the widest point
  • Neck — just below the larynx (Adam's apple), sloping downward

About the US Navy Method

The US Navy formula estimates body fat from circumference measurements. It was developed for military fitness assessments and is widely used as a reliable field measurement tool.

Formula (men): 495 / (1.0324 − 0.19077×log10(waist−neck) + 0.15456×log10(height)) − 450

Formula (women): 495 / (1.29579 − 0.35004×log10(waist+hip−neck) + 0.22100×log10(height)) − 450

Frequently Asked Questions
How accurate is this estimate?

Generally within 3–4% of DEXA scan results. For clinical accuracy, consult a healthcare professional.

Is my data sent anywhere?

No. All calculations run in your browser. Nothing is sent to any server.
