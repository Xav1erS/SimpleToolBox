How to Use Image Tint

  1. Upload a photo by dragging or clicking the drop zone.
  2. Pick a tint color from the palette or enter a HEX value.
  3. Adjust opacity to blend the tint with the original.
  4. Download your tinted image instantly.

Frequently Asked Questions
Is my image uploaded to a server?

No. The tint is applied using the Canvas API in your browser. Your image never leaves your device.

What is a color tint?

A tint overlays a semi-transparent color on an image. Lower opacity blends gently; higher opacity creates a strong color wash.
