How to Add Noise to an Image

  1. Upload a photo by dragging or clicking the drop zone.
  2. Adjust the Intensity slider to control the grain strength.
  3. Choose Monochrome (grey grain) or Color (RGB noise).
  4. Download the result instantly.

Frequently Asked Questions
Is my image uploaded?

No. Everything runs in your browser via Canvas API.

Monochrome vs Color noise?

Monochrome adds grey grain only. Color noise adds independent random variation to R, G, B channels.
