Frequently Asked Questions
How do I lock a color?

Click the 🔓 icon on a card. It turns blue when locked. Locked colors are preserved when you generate new ones.

Can I press a key to generate?

Yes. Press Spacebar to regenerate unlocked colors instantly.

How do I copy a color?

Click the HEX, RGB, or HSL value on any card to copy it to clipboard.
