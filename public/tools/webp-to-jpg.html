What is a WebP to JPG Converter?

A WebP to JPG converter transforms WebP images into JPEG or PNG format — formats that are universally supported by image editors, email clients, and older software. WebP is a modern format developed by Google with excellent compression, but many programs (Adobe Photoshop, older versions of Windows Photo Viewer, email attachments) still do not natively open .webp files. This converter runs entirely in your browser; your files are never uploaded.

How to Convert WebP to JPG

  1. Drop your WebP image onto the preview area, or click to browse and select a file.
  2. Choose the output format: JPEG (smaller, universal) or PNG (lossless, transparency).
  3. For JPEG, adjust the quality slider to balance file size and image quality.
  4. The file size comparison updates in real time.
  5. Click Download to save the converted file.

Frequently Asked Questions

Why can't I open WebP files in Photoshop or Paint?

Older versions of Photoshop and Windows Paint do not have native WebP support. Converting to JPEG or PNG gives you a universally compatible file that opens in any image editor.

Will the JPEG look the same as the WebP?

At 85% quality or above, the visual difference is minimal and imperceptible for most images. WebP and JPEG both use lossy compression, so the output will be very close to the original.

Should I use JPEG or PNG output?

Use JPEG for photos and general images — it produces smaller files. Use PNG if the WebP image has a transparent background and you need to preserve that transparency, or if you need a lossless copy.

Can I convert JPEG back to WebP?

Yes. Use the WebP Converter to convert JPEG, PNG, or any other format to WebP.
