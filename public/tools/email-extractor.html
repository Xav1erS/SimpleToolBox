What is Email Extractor?

Email Extractor scans pasted text and pulls out strings that look like email addresses. It is useful for copied webpages, logs, exports, CRM notes, and any messy text where you need a clean email list fast.

How to Use Email Extractor

  1. Paste the text that contains one or more email addresses.
  2. Choose whether to remove duplicates, sort the results, or lowercase the output.
  3. Review the extracted list and match counters instantly.
  4. Copy the cleaned email list for your spreadsheet, CRM, or audit workflow.

Frequently Asked Questions
Does it validate whether the email address actually exists?

No. It extracts strings that match common email patterns, but it does not verify inbox existence or deliverability.

Can I remove duplicates automatically?

Yes. The Remove duplicates option is enabled by default, so repeated addresses collapse into one line.

Can I lowercase all results?

Yes. Turn on Convert to lowercase if you want the output normalized before copying it elsewhere.

Is my text uploaded anywhere?

No. The extraction runs entirely in your browser and the pasted content stays on your device.
