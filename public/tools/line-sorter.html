What is Line Sorter?

Line Sorter orders one-item-per-line text lists alphabetically or in reverse. It can also use natural numeric sorting, which makes mixed text like item 2 and item 10 sort in a more intuitive order.

How to Use Line Sorter

  1. Paste the list you want to sort into the input area.
  2. Choose A-Z or Z-A order and set the sorting options you need.
  3. Review the sorted lines instantly in the output panel.
  4. Copy the final list back into your document, sheet, or script.

Frequently Asked Questions
What is natural sorting?

Natural sorting treats numbers inside text more like humans expect, so item 2 comes before item 10.

Can I sort in reverse order?

Yes. Change the sort order to Z-A to reverse the result.

Does it ignore case?

Yes. Ignore case is enabled by default, and you can turn it off if you need exact uppercase and lowercase ordering.

Is the sorting done locally?

Yes. Everything runs entirely in your browser.
