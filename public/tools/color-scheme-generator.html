Color Harmony Types
  • Complementary — opposite colors, high contrast
  • Analogous — adjacent colors, harmonious and calm
  • Triadic — three evenly spaced colors, vibrant
  • Split Complementary — base + two colors adjacent to its complement
  • Tetradic — four colors forming a rectangle on the wheel
  • Monochromatic — shades and tints of one hue
