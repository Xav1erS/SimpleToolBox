How to Estimate Reading Time

Paste your text into the box above. The tool counts words and divides by your chosen reading speed (default 200 WPM). Adjust the slider to match fast readers (350+ WPM) or slow/educational content (100–150 WPM).

Frequently Asked Questions
What WPM should I use?

200 WPM is average adult reading. Adjust to 150 for technical content, 250–350 for fast readers, or 130 for speaking time.

Is my text stored?

No. Everything runs in your browser. Your text never leaves your device.
