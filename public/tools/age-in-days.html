    What is Age in Days?

    Age in Days Calculator counts the exact number of days you have been alive, plus days to your next birthday and other milestones. It runs entirely in your browser with no data sent to any server.

    How to Use Age in Days

    1. Enter your birth date.
    2. The "As of" date defaults to today —change it to any date.
    3. Your age in days, weeks, months, hours, and upcoming milestones appear instantly.
