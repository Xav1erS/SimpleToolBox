What is GPA Calculator?

GPA Calculator lets you enter each course, its letter grade, and its credit hours. It converts letter grades to the standard 4.0 grade-point scale, weights each grade by credit hours, and calculates your cumulative GPA instantly.

How to Use GPA Calculator

  1. Enter the course name (optional), letter grade, and credit hours for each course.
  2. Click 'Add Course' to add more rows as needed.
  3. Read your GPA, total credits, and total grade points from the result cards.
  4. The GPA bar shows your score visually on the 0.0–4.0 scale.

Frequently Asked Questions
What grade point scale does this use?

The standard US 4.0 scale: A/A+ = 4.0, A− = 3.7, B+ = 3.3, B = 3.0, B− = 2.7, C+ = 2.3, C = 2.0, C− = 1.7, D+ = 1.3, D = 1.0, D− = 0.7, F = 0.0.

How is weighted GPA calculated?

Each course grade is converted to grade points, multiplied by credit hours, then divided by total credit hours. This gives a credit-weighted average.

Can I add more than the default number of courses?

Yes. Click 'Add Course' to add as many rows as you need.

Does it support plus/minus grades?

Yes. All standard plus and minus letter grades (A+, A, A−, B+, etc.) are supported.
