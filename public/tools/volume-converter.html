What is a Volume Converter?

A volume converter translates a liquid or capacity measurement between different unit systems. Metric units include milliliters (mL), liters (L), and cubic meters (m3). US customary units include fluid ounces, cups, pints, quarts, and gallons. All values use US customary definitions (not UK Imperial).

How to Convert Volume

  1. Type a value into any field (mL, L, m3, fl oz, cup, pt, qt, or gal).
  2. All other fields update instantly.
  3. Use quick example buttons for common cooking and serving sizes.
  4. Click Clear to reset all fields.

Frequently Asked Questions
How many mL in a cup?

1 US cup = 236.588 mL.

How many fluid ounces in a pint?

1 US pint = 16 fluid ounces.

How many cups in a gallon?

1 US gallon = 16 cups.

How many mL in a tablespoon?

1 US tablespoon = 14.787 mL (3 teaspoons).
