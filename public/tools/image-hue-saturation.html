What is Hue & Saturation Adjuster?

Hue & Saturation Adjuster lets you change the color character of any image without a desktop editor. Shift hue to swap color tones globally, raise saturation to make colors more vivid, lower it to desaturate toward grayscale, and adjust lightness to brighten or darken the result.

How to Use Hue & Saturation Adjuster

  1. Upload or drop an image into the tool.
  2. Drag the Hue Rotation slider to shift colors across the spectrum.
  3. Use the Saturation slider to make colors more or less intense.
  4. Use the Lightness slider to brighten or darken the image.
  5. Click Download Edited PNG when the preview looks right.

Frequently Asked Questions
Is my image uploaded to a server?

No. All processing happens entirely in your browser using the Canvas API. Your image never leaves your device.

Which image formats are supported?

Any format your browser can decode: JPG, PNG, WebP, GIF, AVIF, BMP, and more. The download is always saved as a PNG.

Will adjusting hue and saturation degrade image quality?

The tool works at the pixel level converting RGB to HSL and back. Small adjustments have minimal quality impact. The output is saved as lossless PNG so there is no additional compression loss.

What do hue, saturation, and lightness mean?

Hue is the color wheel angle —shifting it changes all colors at once (e.g., red becomes orange or purple). Saturation controls color intensity —higher values make colors more vivid, lower values make them grayer. Lightness shifts the overall brightness of the image toward white or black.
