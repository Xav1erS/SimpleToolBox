Frequently Asked Questions
Is GeoIP precise enough to find a home address?

No —city-level accuracy means within 25—0 km. GeoIP is never precise enough for individual location tracking.

Does it work with IPv6?

Yes —both IPv4 and IPv6 addresses are supported.

What does IP Address Lookup & Geolocation Online —Free IP Info Tool do?
