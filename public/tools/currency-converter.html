What is Currency Converter?

Currency Converter lets you quickly convert between 30+ major world currencies using built-in approximate reference exchange rates. It is designed for fast estimates — travel budgeting, invoice comparisons, or price checks — rather than live financial transactions.

How to Use Currency Converter

  1. Enter the amount you want to convert.
  2. Select the source currency from the first dropdown.
  3. Select the target currency from the second dropdown.
  4. Read the converted result and the exchange rate below.
  5. Click ⇅ to swap the two currencies.

Frequently Asked Questions
Are the rates live?

No. The rates are approximate reference values baked into the tool. For live rates before financial transactions, use your bank or a real-time provider.

Which currencies are supported?

30+ major currencies including USD, EUR, GBP, JPY, CAD, AUD, CHF, CNY, INR, KRW, MXN, BRL, and more.

How do I swap the currencies?

Click the ⇅ button between the two currency selectors to reverse the conversion direction.

Why use approximate rates?

For quick estimates — travel planning, price comparisons, or ballpark figures — approximate rates are sufficient and faster than waiting for a live API.
