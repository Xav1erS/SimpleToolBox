What is Find & Replace?

Find and Replace lets you search for any text pattern and replace every occurrence in one click. It supports plain text, case-insensitive, whole-word, and full JavaScript regex mode. The live preview highlights all matches before you commit the replacement.

How to Use Find & Replace

  1. Paste your text into the Input Text field.
  2. Enter the search term in the Find field.
  3. Enter the replacement text (leave blank to delete matches).
  4. Choose options: case-insensitive, whole word, or regex.
  5. Click Replace All to apply. Copy the result from the Result field.

Frequently Asked Questions
Does it support regular expressions?

Yes. Enable Regex mode and enter any valid JavaScript regular expression. The replacement field supports capture group references like $1.

How do I delete all matches without replacing?

Leave the Replace with field empty and click Replace All. Every match will be removed.

What does whole-word mode do?

It adds word boundary anchors around your pattern so it only matches complete words. For example, cat will not match concatenate.

Is the text sent anywhere?

No. Everything runs locally in your browser.
