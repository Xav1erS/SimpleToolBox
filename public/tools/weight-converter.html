What is a Weight Converter?

A weight converter translates a mass value between different units. Kilograms (kg) are the SI base unit used in most countries. Pounds (lbs) and ounces (oz) are used in the US and UK. Stone is used in the UK for body weight (1 stone = 14 lbs). Grams (g) are used for small masses. Metric tons (t) equal 1,000 kg and are used in industry and freight.

Frequently Asked Questions
How many pounds is 1 kilogram?

1 kilogram = 2.20462 pounds. To convert kg to lbs, multiply by 2.20462.

How many ounces in a pound?

There are 16 ounces in 1 pound (avoirdupois).

What is a stone in kg?

1 stone = 6.35029 kg = 14 pounds. Stone is used in the UK and Ireland for body weight.

How do I convert grams to ounces?

Divide grams by 28.3495. For example, 100 g ÷ 28.3495 = 3.527 oz.
