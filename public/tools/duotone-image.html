What is Duotone Image?

Duotone Image transforms a photo into a two-color version by mapping shadows and highlights to custom colors. It is useful for posters, campaigns, branding experiments, and any edit that needs a stronger graphic identity than the original photo.

How to Use Duotone Image

  1. Upload one image into the tool.
  2. Choose a shadow color and a highlight color.
  3. Review the duotone preview instantly.
  4. Download the edited PNG when it looks right.

Frequently Asked Questions
What is a duotone effect?

A duotone effect maps the darker and lighter parts of an image to two chosen colors instead of using the original full-color photo.

Can I choose my own two colors?

Yes. Set one color for shadows and one color for highlights.

Does this work best on photos?

Yes. Photos, portraits, and high-contrast screenshots usually show the effect most clearly.

Is the image uploaded anywhere?

No. The duotone effect runs entirely in your browser.
