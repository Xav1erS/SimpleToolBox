What is Percentage Calculator?

Percentage Calculator provides three instant calculators: find what X% of a number equals, determine what percentage one number is of another, and compute the percentage change between an old and new value. All three update instantly as you type.

How to Use Percentage Calculator

  1. Choose the calculation type: percentage of a number, percentage ratio, or percentage change.
  2. Enter the numbers in the appropriate fields.
  3. The result updates instantly as you type.
  4. Click Copy next to any result to copy it to your clipboard.

Frequently Asked Questions
How do I calculate 20% of 150?

Use the first calculator: enter 20 in the percent field and 150 in the number field. The result is 30.

How do I find what percentage 30 is of 150?

Use the second calculator: enter 30 as X and 150 as Y. The result is 20%.

What is percentage change?

Percentage change measures how much a value increased or decreased relative to its original value. A positive result means growth; a negative result means a decrease.

Can it handle decimals?

Yes. All three calculators accept decimal inputs and display up to six decimal places when needed.
