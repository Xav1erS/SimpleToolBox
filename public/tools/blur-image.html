What is an Online Image Blur Tool?

An online image blur tool applies a Gaussian blur filter to your photo — softening edges, hiding sensitive content, or creating artistic effects. Gaussian blur is the most natural-looking blur, mimicking a camera's depth-of-field effect. Common use cases include blurring faces or license plates for privacy, softening backgrounds, and creating dreamy photo effects. This tool processes images entirely in your browser using the HTML5 Canvas API — your images are never uploaded.

How to Blur an Image Online

  1. Drop your image onto the preview area, or click to browse and select a file.
  2. Adjust the Blur Radius slider — or use the presets: Soft (2px), Medium (8px), Privacy (30px).
  3. The canvas preview updates in real time as you adjust the slider.
  4. Choose your output format: JPEG (smaller) or PNG (lossless).
  5. Click Download to save the blurred image.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I blur only part of the image (e.g., just a face)?

This tool applies blur to the entire image. For selective/regional blurring, you would need image editing software (e.g., GIMP, Photoshop, Canva) that supports layer masks or selection tools. However, this tool is useful for blurring background images entirely or applying an artistic blur to the whole photo.

What blur radius should I use for privacy purposes?

For effective privacy blur that obscures recognizable details (faces, license plates, screen content), a radius of 20–40px is recommended. The "Privacy" preset at 30px provides strong anonymization for most images.

Does blurring an image reduce file size?

Often yes — blurred images have less detail and more uniform color areas, which makes JPEG compression more effective. A heavily blurred image can be 30–50% smaller than the original at the same quality setting.

Can I undo the blur?

Once downloaded, blur cannot be reversed — blurring permanently destroys the image detail in those areas. The original file in your browser is not modified; just reload the page and upload the original again.
