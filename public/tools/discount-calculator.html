What is Discount Calculator?

Discount Calculator provides two calculators: the first finds the sale price and savings from an original price and a discount percentage; the second works in reverse 鈥?given the original and sale price, it calculates what discount was applied.

How to Use Discount Calculator

  1. To find the sale price: enter the original price and the discount percentage.
  2. Read the sale price, amount saved, and the percentage of the original you are paying.
  3. To find the discount %: enter the original price and the sale price in the second calculator.
  4. Read the discount percentage and the amount saved.
