What is Reading Time Estimator?

Reading Time Estimator calculates how long it takes to read a piece of text based on an average reading speed. Paste any article, essay, or document to instantly see its estimated reading time.

How to Use Reading Time Estimator

Paste your text into the box above. The tool counts words and divides by your chosen reading speed (default 200 WPM). Adjust the slider to match fast readers (350+ WPM) or slow/educational content (100—50 WPM).

Frequently Asked Questions
What WPM should I use?

200 WPM is average adult reading. Adjust to 150 for technical content, 250—50 for fast readers, or 130 for speaking time.

Is my text stored?

No. Everything runs in your browser. Your text never leaves your device.
