How to Remove Line Breaks

  1. Paste your text into the input box.
  2. Choose what to replace line breaks with (space, nothing, comma, or custom).
  3. Toggle "Keep paragraph breaks" to preserve blank-line separators.
  4. Click Remove Line Breaks and copy or use the result.

Frequently Asked Questions
Is my text sent to a server?

No. Everything runs in your browser. Your text never leaves your device.

What does 'Keep paragraph breaks' do?

When enabled, double line breaks (blank lines) are preserved as paragraph separators, while single line breaks within paragraphs are removed.
