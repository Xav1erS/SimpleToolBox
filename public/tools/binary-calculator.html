How Binary Arithmetic Works

Binary numbers use only 0 and 1. Addition carries at 2 (1+1=10 in binary). The calculator converts both inputs to decimal, performs the operation, then converts the result back to binary, hexadecimal, and octal for display.

Frequently Asked Questions
What is the maximum input size?

Up to 52 bits (JavaScript's safe integer limit). For larger numbers, use a BigInt-based tool.
