Tip Etiquette Guide
  • 10–12% — below average or poor service
  • 15–18% — standard or good service
  • 20%+ — excellent service or fine dining
  • 25%+ — exceptional service or celebration
Frequently Asked Questions
Is the tip calculated before or after tax?

This calculator applies the tip to the bill amount you enter. If you include tax in the bill amount, the tip is calculated on the after-tax total.
