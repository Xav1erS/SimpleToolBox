What is Text Statistics?

Text Statistics tool analyzes any text and shows detailed metrics: word count, character count, sentence count, paragraph count, average word length, reading time, and more.

What Text Statistics Are Shown?

  • Words —total non-whitespace tokens
  • Characters —total including spaces
  • Sentences —counted by .!? punctuation
  • Paragraphs —blocks separated by blank lines
  • Reading Time —at 200 WPM average
  • Unique Words —distinct lowercase tokens

How to Use Text Statistics

  1. Paste or type any text into the input area.
  2. All statistics update instantly as you type.
  3. Review metrics including word count, character count, sentence count, and reading time.
  4. Copy individual values as needed for your analysis.

Frequently Asked Questions
Is my text sent to a server?

No. All analysis runs in your browser. Your text never leaves your device.

How are unique words counted?

Words are lowercased and deduplicated. "The" and "the" count as one unique word.
