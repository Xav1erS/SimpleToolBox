Roman Numeral Rules

Symbols: I=1, V=5, X=10, L=50, C=100, D=500, M=1000.

Subtractive notation is used for IV, IX, XL, XC, CD, and CM. Standard modern usage typically covers values from 1 to 3999.

Frequently Asked Questions
What is the largest number supported?

3999 (MMMCMXCIX). Roman numerals have no standard representation for 4000+.

Are lowercase Roman numerals accepted?

Yes — both uppercase (XIV) and lowercase (xiv) input are accepted.
