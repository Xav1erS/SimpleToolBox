Frequently Asked Questions

Is this service free?

Yes, all tools on Simple Toolbox are completely free to use. We believe in making useful utilities accessible to everyone without cost barriers.

Do I need to register?

No registration or login is required to access our utilities. We respect your privacy and want to make our tools as accessible as possible.

Is my data safe?

We do not store any of the data you process through our tools. All processing happens locally in your browser, ensuring your information remains private and secure.

How long does it take to get a response?

We aim to respond to all inquiries within 24-48 hours during business days. For urgent matters, please email us directly at support@simpletoolbox.dev.
